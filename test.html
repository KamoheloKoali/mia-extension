MIA Extension Test Page

Testing Instructions:

  1. Load the extension in Chrome by going to chrome://extensions/
  2. Enable "Developer mode" in the top right
  3. Click "Load unpacked" and select the /home/bad-dev/mia-extension/dist folder
  4. Click on the MIA extension icon in the toolbar
  5. Click "START RECORDING" to begin recording
  6. A floating recording control should appear on this page
  7. You can drag the floating control around
  8. Click "STOP" on either the floating control or in the popup to stop recording
